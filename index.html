About Me

Born in Oklahoma City, I grew up on classic rock, football and Mexican food. After college, I wanted a different scene, so I packed up my Toyota Corrola and moved to Seattle, where I knew no one and had no job waiting. After odd jobs in the art world, I fell into hospitality at the Sheraton Seattle. I spent the next twelve years managing aspects of hotel operations, which would bring me the opportunity to relocate to Washginton, D.C.

I have been in The District for three years now and, in the unique, hustling environment, I decided to venture into the world of Full Stack Web development. Here's a glimpse into who I am and what I do.
